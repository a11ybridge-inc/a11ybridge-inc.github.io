What sets us apart

We're not just consultants — we're advocates for digital equity in every market we serve.

Leading expertise

3+ years of hands-on WCAG auditing, assistive technology testing, and accessibility consulting across sectors.

Customisable solutions

Every engagement is scoped to your team's needs — from quick pulse checks to full organizational transformation programs.

Global policy understanding

Deep knowledge of WCAG 2.2, EN 301 549, Section 508, and emerging regional frameworks relevant to GCC and African markets.
