Assessment & Auditing

Accessibility Assessments

Get actionable advice to improve your digital products.

We provide comprehensive WCAG 2.2 audits, ongoing monitoring, and targeted pulse checks that identify barriers and prioritize fixes. Our assessments cover web applications, mobile apps, documents, and digital content — with practical, prioritized remediation guidance your team can act on immediately.

  • Full WCAG 2.2 Level A, AA conformance audits
  • Assistive technology testing (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver)
  • Document and content accessibility reviews
  • Ongoing monitoring and pulse check programs

Compliance Documentation

Accessibility Conformance Reports

Share your current level of accessibility conformance.

An Accessibility Conformance Report (ACR), based on a VPAT®, is an official document that shows the outcomes of a conformance evaluation against recognized standards. ACRs are increasingly required by governments and enterprise procurement processes across the GCC and beyond.

  • VPAT® 2.x conformance evaluation
  • WCAG 2.2, Section 508, and EN 301 549 criteria
  • Procurement-ready documentation
  • Periodic updates as your product evolves
